About

Annie Hayes

Annie Hayes is an indie-folk artist and avid songwriter. She draws inspiration from many artists like The Sundays and the Cocteau Twins, and has cultivated a following in the south coast of England.

Her debut single, 'Clay', got a spot on BBC Introducing, and hit Number 1 on the South Coast Music Chart. Annie's recent single, 'Picture Perfect', featured at second on the chart.
